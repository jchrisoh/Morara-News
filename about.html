 About Us 

Morara News is a platform in which student writers are able to comfortably share their opinions on current events and issues impacting students.

This way, we hope to broaden the reach of the voices of students and inform our readers about diverse thoughts and ideas. At the moment, Morara News is not accepting any new student writers. If you are interested in joining our team, please check our website next time for any new updates.

Another pivotal aspect of Morara News is our student interviews. Through interviews, our team endeavors to help students by offering them a way to listen to other studentsâ€™ advice and experiences. In the past, we have conducted interviews with senior high school students to aid younger students in their post-secondary application processes.

 Videos 

Through videos, we wish to provide content that is relatable to students from all different backgrounds and experiences. Our videos feature a variety of content, such as summaries of our articles, student interviews and experiences, cooking tutorials, extracurricular life, and many more.  Note About Political Articles 

Our news articles are neither meant to endorse any candidate nor any political party, but is meant for the sole purpose of offering insight for students interested in the political process and sharing diverse viewpoints. Furthermore, political opinions contained or mentioned in these articles may not align with those of Vancouver Student Voices.

Morara News, previously known as Vancouver Student Voices, is a student–led and independent news platform, in which students are able to comfortably share their opinions on current events and issues impacting them. This way, Morara News hopes to broaden the reach of student voices and inform our readers about diverse thoughts and ideas.
