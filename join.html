Welcome to  Morara News 

Thank you for your interest in joining our team. Our goal is to strengthen students' knowledge of current events and encourage independent thought. Morara News hopes to help all students learn more about their environment and have students gain a platform to share their perspectives on issues that affect them.

Unfortunately, Morara News is currently not accepting any new student writers. If you are interested in joining our team, please check back later for any new updates.

We welcome any comments and suggestions you may have. Please do not hesitate to ask any questions or contact us: moraranews@gmail.com.

Morara News, previously known as Vancouver Student Voices, is a student–led and independent news platform, in which students are able to comfortably share their opinions on current events and issues impacting them. This way, Morara News hopes to broaden the reach of student voices and inform our readers about diverse thoughts and ideas.
