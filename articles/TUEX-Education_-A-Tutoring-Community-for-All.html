TUEX Education: A Tutoring Community for All
By Sean and Chris Oh | Published Mar 28, 2021 6:48 p.m. PST
What is TUEX Education?
Based in Vancouver, TUEX Education is an online platform that matches students with experienced tutors. Unlike other tutoring platforms, TUEX gives tutors the freedom to set their own prices, and students likewise are given the flexibility to choose their tutors, depending on their budget, needs, preferred languages, and more.

The TUEX platform is home to over 1000 tutors, ranging from high school students to PhD candidates at the University of British Columbia, Simon Fraser University, the University of Victoria, the University of Toronto, and the University of Waterloo. TUEX, which considers child protection of utmost importance, requires that its tutors complete a criminal record check before they are allowed to teach students.

What can students gain from TUEX Education?
The Covid-19 pandemic has resulted in an increased number of students seeking additional support with their academic work. Unsurprisingly, TUEX, with its pricing as low as $13/hour, has noticed a spike in tutoring rates as a result. Not only does TUEX offer tutoring in a wide variety of traditional subject areas, such as math and essay writing, but also in piano, driving, and many more. With TUEX, students can find exactly what they need to excel in school and out.

In addition to customizable tutoring services, TUEX provides students the opportunity to generate income by becoming tutors themselves. Some of the best tutors are students sharing their expertise.

How does TUEX work?
Students can download the TUEX app from the App Store or on Google Play. Once in the app, students can create a free account and browse tutors and classes. Post selection of a tutor (based on subject matter, price, and language preference), students can schedule their class or classes. Tutor sessions can be held over various platforms, such as FaceTime, Zoom, Skype, or Google Hangouts.

TUEX Foundation: Helping Low-income Students?
The TUEX Foundation was created in 2018 to assist low-income students who are unable to afford tutors. The TUEX Foundation offers financial support through tutoring coupons that can be used on the TUEX app. This financial support ensures equitable access to education, regardless of socioeconomic background.

TUEX Education is a safe, affordable and inclusive educational platform. Whether you need a Mandarin speaking math tutor to prepare you for your grade 12 exams, or an English-speaking tutor that can equip you with graphic design basics, TUEX is your solution.
