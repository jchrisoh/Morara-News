Movies and TV Shows to Watch in 2020
By Chloe E.C. | Published Nov 17, 2020 12:07 p.m. PST
1. Little Fires Everywhere 2020 TV series, based on the novel by Celeste Ng (2020)
Type: TV Series
Brief Description: Little Fires Everywhere is a TV series based on a novel published in 2017 by author Celeste Ng. The story takes place in the 1990s in an area known as Shaker Heights, where two families reside in. You can find this TV series on Hulu.

2. The Baby Sitters Club based on the books by Ann M. Martin (2020)
Type: TV Series (Remake)
Brief Description: The Baby-Sitters Club is comedy-drama TV series that was released on July 3rd, 2020. The TV series is based on the book series written by Ann M. Martin and can be viewed on Netflix.

3. Leave No Trace (2018)
Type: Film
Brief Description: Released in the United Kingdom on June 29, 2018, Leave No Trace describes the struggles and challenges that a father and daughter are forced to overcome. You can find the movie on Youtube.

4. Jojo Rabbit (2019)
Type: Film
Brief Description: Jojo Rabbit, which was released on September 8, 2019, features Scarlett Johansson, Taika Waititi, and Roman Griffin Davis. Jojo, who is a lonely German boy with a firm belief in Nazism, finds out that a Jewish girl is being hidden in his house by his mother.

5. Space Force (2020)
Type: TV Series
Brief Description: Space Force is a comedy television series created by Greg Daniels and Steve Carell. The television series presents a group of people whose task is to create the sixth branch of the United States Armed Forces.

6. Never Have I Ever (2020)
Type: TV Series
Brief Description: Never Have I Ever is a comedy-drama television series that revolves around Devi Vishwakumar, an Indian-American high school student, who wants to return to a normal life after a rough freshman year.

7. Black Panther (2018)
Type: Film
Brief Description: Black Panther was released on January 29, 2018, and broke box office records with an overall box office of $1.344 billion dollars. The film describes T’Challa’s multiple obstacles in inheriting the throne and protecting his nation, Wakanda.

8. Tenet (2020)
Type: Film
Brief Description: Tenet, a film released on August 26, 2020, is a science fiction film that is full of action and thrill. The film is about a CIA agent who participates in a secret mission to prevent World War III.

9. Little Women (2019)
Type: Film
Brief Description: Little Women was released on December 7, 2019 and is a contemporary drama film written and directed by Greta Gerwig. The film is set in New England during the American Civil War and centres four sisters and their mother who try to find their place in the world.

10. The Good Place (2016)
Type: TV Series (4 Seasons)
Brief Description: The Good Place is a fantasy comedy television series created by Michael Schur. The film presents The Good Place, which is a town that deceased people go to if they have been good in their lives, and revolves around Eleanor who arrives at The Good Place, but realizes she is mistaken for somebody else.
